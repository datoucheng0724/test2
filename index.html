🗺️ Product Roadmap 🗺️ 產品藍圖

2022–23
Performance & Specialisation效能提升與產品特化
  • OLED & Creator PC lines expanded; dual-screen ultra-thin models launchedOLED 與創作者電腦產品線擴張；雙螢幕超薄高效機型上市
  • Multi-phase power motherboards & high-end 3D graphics cards developed多相供電主機板與高階3D顯示卡開發
  • Early AI server solutions for smart manufacturing & healthcare早期 AI 伺服器應用於智慧製造與醫療
2024
The AI TransformationAI 轉型元年
  • 1st-generation AI Laptops launched — redefining user behaviour via generative AI第一代 AI 筆電上市，以生成式 AI 重新定義使用行為
  • ROG Ally handheld ecosystem expanded alongside gaming laptopsROG Ally 掌上型電玩生態系擴展
  • AI Server revenue grew >50% YoYAI 伺服器年增逾50%
2025
Ubiquitous AI & Sustainability全面 AI 化與永續發展
  • Copilot+ PCs & AI-integrated motherboards; target >30% Copilot+ PC market shareCopilot+ PC 與 AI 整合主機板；目標 Copilot+ PC 市占率逾30%
  • Liquid-cooled AI supercomputers & high-scalability AI servers for cloud/edge液冷 AI 超級電腦與高擴展性 AI 伺服器，應對雲端與邊緣運算需求
  • Low-carbon product design & sustainable development integrated into R&D (ESG)低碳產品設計與永續發展納入研發核心（ESG）
